Nutritional exercises for the month of Ramadan

Exercises for the month of Ramadan

  • Running: 30 minutes in the morning
  • Strength exercises: 3 sets of 12 repetitions of push-ups, bends, and extensions
  • Balance exercises: 3 sets of 30 seconds each
  • Running in place: 20 minutes after breakfast
  • Flexibility exercises: 10 minutes of stretching in the evening

Diet for the month of Ramadan

This system includes three main meals and a snack:

  • Breakfast: vegetable soup + steak or chicken + ½ cup of rice + 1 cup of milk + two dates
  • Snack: 1 cup of yogurt + 1 banana
  • Dinner: vegetable salad + steak or chicken + ½ cup of rice + 1 cup of juice
  • Suhoor: 1 cup of milk + 2 boiled eggs + bread + two dates

You should avoid fatty and salty foods, carbonated drinks and drink plenty of water.

Ramadan supplications

O Allah, I seek refuge in You from worry and grief, incapacity and laziness, miserliness and cowardice, the burden of debt and from being overpowered by men.
Oh God, stop me with your permission from your forbidden, and enrich me with your grace from those other than you
Our Lord, do not take us, if we forget us, or make us, our Lord, and do not carry us with insulting, as you have carried it to those who are not, for us, and for us, and for us, and for us, for us
My Lord, enable me to be grateful for Your favor which You have bestowed upon me and upon my parents, and to do righteous deeds that please You, and admit me, by Your mercy, among Your righteous servants.
Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and save us from the punishment of the Fire
Our Lord, turn away from us the torment of Hell, for its torment was a love
My Lord, make me an establisher of prayer and of my offspring, our Lord, and accept my supplication
Lord grant me judgment and join me with the righteous
Our Lord, grant us mercy from Yourself and provide us with guidance from our affairs
